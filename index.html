Did you know that computers only understand 0s and 1s? The binary system (base-2) is the foundation of all modern computing. Every number, letter, and symbol on your screen is just a combination of 0s and 1s behind the scenes!

Bin2Dec is a simple tool that helps you convert binary numbers into decimal format instantly. Just enter a sequence of 0s and 1s (up to 8 digits), and watch the magic happen!
